How It Works

Within distribution, a major issue in socio-economic philosophy, commodities, credit, inventory, rewards and any other that can be expressed in quantity, the mechanism works.

The criterion of only two factors to qualify as prime makes scarcity of the same as we move up the number line thus, one notices that there are 15 primes for the first 50 counting numbers and 10 for the next 50. This tendency continues but perhaps not in a linear fashion.

The effect becomes that as we move one prime to another, the distance along the number line between two primes increases. This distance in units of counting numbers is called a prime gap.

For example the distance between the 10th and 9th prime is 29-23=6 while that between the 5th and 4th prime is 11-7=4. These variegated and somewhat bulging gaps are the cornerstone of applicability of prime numbers in distribution

Basis

The whole is greater than the sum of its parts



Since 7 is greater than 0, with primes atleast, the whole is greater than the sum of its parts. If we were doing pure mathematics, we would take this as a conjecture, that is, an incomplete solution until we have tried out all the ordinals of primes in reality or abstraction; but here we are simply applying mathematics.

Application - Commodity

Nominal->Quantity || Ordinal->Quality

Someone with a worth of 15 ie 47 items can distribute to two other with respective worth of 5 and 10 giving them 11 and 29 items respectively whilst he is left with a balance of 7 items. This balance he could dispose off at a worth of 4. Yes, a balance, that is the return of distribution, not exactly a profit since there is no mark up, neither an interest since there is no added proportion. It is an automatic distributive mechanism which can be generalized into an economic activity. Well, calculating, the return from the transaction is about 26%(4/15) which at par or even above prevalent return on investment. Simply put, it is an alternative to appropriation such that if we could relegate the latter to automatons, we could pursue the former in equivalent or much more satisfaction.

Application - Credit

Ordinal -> Principal | Nominal -> Maturity

when given a principal of 50 units of commodity, usually designated as capital and/or technology, then the time is counted in a timeframe, that 229(50th prime) units of time as its maturity. Thus, fractions of maturity timeframe will always remain as balance/return when this original principal is further redistributed. Redistributing the original loan of 50 units to two persons, given 20 and 30 units respectively, the average maturity of the redistributed loans shrinks from the original 229 to (113+71)/2 = 92 units of time; meaning the recovered amounts could be redistributed the second time before the maturity of the original loan is due. (229/92) is approximately 2.5. A principal of 50 yields (229-50)/2 = 89.5 gross or 39.5 net while 30 and 20 yield (113-30)/2 = 41.5 gross or 11.5 net and (71-20)/2=25.5 gross or 5.5 net. Gross or net multiplied by the repetitions i.e (5.5+11.5)*2.5 = 42.5 which is greater than 39.5 to return to the first lender.
